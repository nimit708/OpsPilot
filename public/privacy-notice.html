OPSPILOT PRIVACY NOTICE

Workplace-data transparency

This notice is a product template. Your employer must replace controller, DPO, lawful-basis, retention, transfer, and complaint details before rollout.

Purpose

OpsPilot processes approved workplace sources to propose Jira tickets, incident communications, email notifications, Confluence documentation, and employee-controlled daily summaries. It must not be used for covert monitoring, productivity scoring, performance evaluation, emotion inference, or disciplinary profiling.

Data processed

Identity, work email, approved-channel messages, explicitly supplied meeting transcripts, operational decisions, approvals, integration identifiers, and security audit logs. Private, HR, union, medical, legal, and unrelated personal conversations must be excluded.

Human control

External actions and Confluence publications require an authorized human approver. Employees can request access, correction, restriction, objection, or deletion through the privacy API or their designated privacy contact.

Retention and access

Operational content defaults to 30 days unless a documented schedule requires otherwise. Access is role-based and logged. Secrets and message bodies are redacted from monitoring logs.

Your rights

Depending on applicable law, individuals may have rights of access, correction, erasure, restriction, objection, portability, and complaint to a supervisory authority. Withdrawing optional consent must not cause detriment.
