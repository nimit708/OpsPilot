# Data Protection Impact Assessment — OpsPilot Template

1. Controller, owner, DPO/privacy contact, assessment date and reviewers.
2. Business problem and why monitoring/AI is necessary.
3. Exact sources, channels, people, data fields, frequency and data-flow diagram.
4. Outputs and recipients: Jira, Slack/Teams, Outlook, Confluence, OpenAI and monitoring stack.
5. Lawful basis for each purpose; special-category/criminal-offence data assessment.
6. Worker consultation, notice, genuine choices and alternatives.
7. Necessity and proportionality; less intrusive alternatives considered.
8. Risks: overcollection, function creep, inaccurate routing, bias, chilling effects, unauthorized access, transfers, retention and automated action.
9. Controls: allowlists, exclusions, minimization, redaction, human approval, RBAC, audit, encryption, retention, request handling, vendor terms and incident response.
10. Residual risk, risk owner, actions, approval decision and review date.

Do not launch until high residual risks are accepted by the authorized privacy function and any required supervisory consultation is complete.
